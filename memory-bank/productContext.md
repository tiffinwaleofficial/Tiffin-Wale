# Tiffin-Wale Product Context

## Why This Project Exists

### Market Problem
The traditional food delivery model fails to address the specific needs of students and bachelors who require:
- **Reliable daily meals** at predictable times
- **Cost-effective solutions** for regular food consumption
- **Dietary flexibility** and meal customization
- **Consistent quality** and portion control

### Solution Approach
Tiffin-Wale creates a **subscription-based food ecosystem** that connects local food providers with students/bachelors through a multi-interface platform, ensuring:
- **Predictable demand** for partners
- **Reliable supply** for customers
- **Quality control** through platform oversight
- **Scalable operations** through technology

## Core User Journeys

### Student/Bachelor Journey

#### 1. Discovery & Onboarding
- **Entry Point**: Web app or mobile app
- **First Action**: Browse available meal plans and partner options
- **Key Decision**: Choose subscription plan (daily, weekly, monthly)
- **Success Metric**: Account creation and plan selection

#### 2. Meal Customization
- **Process**: Select dietary preferences, portion sizes, delivery times
- **Personalization**: Dietary restrictions, spice preferences, meal timing
- **Flexibility**: Ability to pause, modify, or cancel subscriptions
- **Success Metric**: Active subscription with customized preferences

#### 3. Daily Usage
- **Morning**: Receive meal suggestions and confirmations
- **Meal Time**: Real-time delivery tracking and notifications
- **Post-Meal**: Rate meals and provide feedback
- **Success Metric**: High satisfaction scores and retention

#### 4. Subscription Management
- **Ongoing**: Modify preferences, pause/resume subscriptions
- **Payment**: Manage payment methods and billing cycles
- **Support**: Access help and resolve issues
- **Success Metric**: Subscription retention and renewal rates

### Partner Journey

#### 1. Partner Onboarding
- **Application**: Submit business details and menu offerings
- **Verification**: Platform review and approval process
- **Setup**: Configure menu, pricing, and delivery capabilities
- **Success Metric**: Active partner status with menu published

#### 2. Daily Operations
- **Order Management**: Receive and process daily orders
- **Preparation**: Prepare meals according to specifications
- **Delivery**: Coordinate delivery logistics and timing
- **Success Metric**: Order fulfillment rate and customer satisfaction

#### 3. Business Growth
- **Analytics**: Track earnings, customer feedback, and performance
- **Menu Optimization**: Adjust offerings based on demand and feedback
- **Expansion**: Scale operations and reach new customers
- **Success Metric**: Revenue growth and customer base expansion

### Admin Journey

#### 1. Platform Oversight
- **Monitoring**: Track system performance and user activity
- **Management**: Oversee partners, customers, and orders
- **Analytics**: Generate reports and insights
- **Success Metric**: Platform stability and user satisfaction

#### 2. Support & Resolution
- **Issue Management**: Handle customer and partner support requests
- **Quality Control**: Monitor food quality and service standards
- **Dispute Resolution**: Mediate conflicts and ensure fair outcomes
- **Success Metric**: Resolution time and user satisfaction
